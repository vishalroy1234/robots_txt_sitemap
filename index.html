Verdict: Hiding the sitemap does not stop a scraper. It only stops a human from clicking a link. An automated scraper will just crawl your categories or guess standard sitemap URLs.

Strategic Recommendation for Indiamart

Based on the analysis of 25+ competitors and Google's official documentation.

1

Add the Sitemap Index URL

Don't list every individual sitemap. Just list your main index file (e.g., https://www.indiamart.com/sitemap_index.xml).

2

Security via Rate Limiting, Not Obscurity

Rely on your WAF (Web Application Firewall) to block scrapers who hit your pages too fast. Hiding the sitemap file is not a valid security control.
